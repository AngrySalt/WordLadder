Valid chain words will count towards your score in endless mode, and can get you closer to your goal word.
Valid chain words must follow these rules:
1: They must be unique, meaning you can't go in an infinite loop using the same 3 words.
2: They must be the same length
3: Your message should contain only a singular word, any special characters or spaces will cause the bot to ignore you.
4: Capitilization does not matter
5: They must have only a one letter difference from the previous word:
Starting word: cat
| ✅ bat is valid
| ✅ ban is valid
| ❌ nat is invalid
6: They must be a valid dictionary entry
Starting Word: cart
| ✅ dart is valid
| ❌ bart is invalid
